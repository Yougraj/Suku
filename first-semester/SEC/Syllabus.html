⚖️ Legal Literacy Syllabus ⚖️

Understanding the legal system with clarity & purpose ✨

📋 Course Information

  • Course Title: Legal Literacy
  • Course Code: SEC107
  • Nature of Course: SEC
  • Total Credits: 3
  • Distribution of Marks: 80 (End-Sem) + 20 (In-Sem)

🎯 Course Objectives

To acquaint student with the structure and manner of functioning of the legal system in India.

📘 Unit I – Legal System in India (20 Marks)

Hours: L = 9, T = 3, Total Hrs = 12

  • ⚖️ An Introduction to the Legal system in India
  • ⚖️ System of courts/tribunals and their jurisdiction in India
  • ⚖️ Criminal and civil courts, writ jurisdiction
  • ⚖️ Specialized courts such as:
    • ✨ Juvenile Justice Boards
    • ✨ Family Courts
    • ✨ Tribunals
  • ⚖️ Alternate dispute mechanisms such as:
    • ✨ Lok-Adalats
    • ✨ Non-formal Mechanisms

📘 Unit II – Preliminary Criminal Procedures (20 Marks)

Hours: L = 9, T = 2, Total Hrs = 11

  • 🔍 Filing a FIR
  • 🔍 Arrest
  • 🔍 Bail
  • 🔍 Search and seizure

📘 Unit III – Laws relating to Gender, Consumer Protection, Cyber Crimes (20 Marks)

Hours: L = 9, T = 2, Total Hrs = 11

  • 👥 Laws relating to dowry
  • 👥 Sexual harassment and domestic violence
  • 👥 Laws relating to consumer rights
  • 👥 Laws relating to cyber crimes

📘 Unit IV – Practical (25 Marks)

Hours: L = 9, T = 2, Total Hrs = 11

  • ⚖️ A Victim of Child Abuses
